Why We Built This

Every Instagram unfollow tracker app we found had the same problem — they all asked for your Instagram login. That's a huge privacy risk and violates Instagram's Terms of Service — see why login-based tools carry real risk.

We built Unfollow Finder differently. Instagram lets you export your own data as JSON files. Our tool reads those files directly in your browser — your data never leaves your device, and we never see your followers or following list. In fact, we only ever ask for two files — read about our two-file approach.

It's the safest way to track unfollowers, and we think it should be the standard. Curious how we stack up against the alternatives? See how we compare to other tools.
