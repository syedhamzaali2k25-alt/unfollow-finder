Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

🔒 TL;DR — Your Instagram data never leaves your device. We process everything locally in your browser. We never see your followers or following list.

1. Who We Are

Unfollow Finder is a privacy-first Instagram unfollow tracker available at unfollowfinder.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

2. What Data We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you create an account
  • Usage data: Number of scans performed (not the content of scans)
  • Payment information: Handled entirely by LemonSqueezy — we never see your card details
  • Analytics: Basic page view data via Google Analytics

3. What Data We Do NOT Collect

❌ We never collect, store, or transmit your Instagram followers or following list. All JSON file processing happens locally in your browser.

  • Your Instagram password
  • Your Instagram followers list
  • Your Instagram following list
  • Any Instagram account credentials

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our service
  • Send important account notifications
  • Process payments via LemonSqueezy
  • Respond to customer support requests

5. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with industry-standard encryption. We use SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.

6. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at: privacy@unfollowfinder.com
